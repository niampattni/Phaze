Playing the Game
Instructions > Playing the Game

To play this game, you will have to match the colored picture on the right side of the black and white picture.

You must choose the correct color, otherwise the program will tell you that the color is wrong, and you will lose some points. If you finish the picture within the time limit, you will go on to the next picture, otherwise you will stop there and see how much points you achieved.
