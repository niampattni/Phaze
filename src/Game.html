Main menu
Instructions > Main menu

On the main menu there are several choices to choose from, instructions, playing the game, exit, and highscores.

When you click play, it asks for input for your username, then gets you to choose a difficulty, then you get to play the game.

When you click instructions, it brings you to a screen with these instructions.

When you click Highscores, it brings you to a screen that lets you see the top ten scores that have been achieved.
When you click Exit, the window closes.
