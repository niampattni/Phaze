How to Play?

The Basics:

When the game screen opens, you will see multiple buttons with colour names on them.
Clicking a button will select a colour until the picture is completed or you click another button.
Once a colour has been selected, proceed to click on the black/white image.
The sections on the image are shown by the black lines. Be sure that when colouring a section that the colour you have selected is the same as the colour for the corresponding section in the coloured image!
Once all 12 pictures have been successfully cleared, the level will be complete!

Scoring:

  • You start with 1000 points
  • Every second you take you lose 1 point
  • If you attempt to use the wrong colour you lose 10 points
  • If you attempt to colour in the black outline you lose 10 points
  • Remaining points are multiplied by the Level number

Level Differences:

Level 1:

  1. Has only primary colouring options
  2. Uses only primary colours

Level 2:

  1. Has primary and secondary colouring options
  2. Uses primary and secondary colours

Level 3:

  1. Has only primary colouring options
  2. Uses only secondary colours (mixing is necessary)
